Excluded members are owners, officers, or LLC members who have filed an exclusion election with the carrier. They are not covered by the Workers’ Comp policy, and their wages are removed from the premium basis — so they are not part of what the carrier charges. Their wages still appear in payroll, but Atlas subtracts them before calculating premium.
